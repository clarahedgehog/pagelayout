About Me

Photo of me:

Welcome to my website! My name is Mira and here is some information about me. I am 34 years old and I currently live in San Myshuno with my niece. I work as a Celebrity Chef but I also own my own café called Espresso Macchiato. Cooking and baking are my favorite hobbies, you can almost always find me in the kitchen with something in the oven smelling wonderful haha. I am also engaged to my amazing Miles and together we have our beautiful son. If you are interested in my life, I sometimes post about it in my blog.

If you are big fan of food, you have come to the right place! I share a lot of amazing recipes, see under "Recipies", and if you do not want to cook or bake them yourself, you can always come by "Espresso Macchiato" and try them! Almost all of my recipes are available to to buy in my café. There you can also read some books, have a quiet moment from the hectic city and if you seem like a nice person, you might even be allowed to take a dip in our backyard pool. See you there!
